Each character in Star Wars RPG has 6 defining Attributes:Brawn(B), Agility (A), Intellect (I), Cunning (C), Willpower (W), and Presence (P) . Brawn is a blend of Brute power, strength and overall toughness. Agility is manual dexterity, hand-eye coordination and body control. Intellect is intelligence, education, mental acuity and ability to rationalize and reason. Cunning is how crafty, devious, clever and creative a character is.
