Careers

Overview

The next step is choosing a career for you character to have in the galaxy. You are unable to change this at any point in your adventure. In addition, you get to pick their starting specialization. While you do get to add specializations later on, you don’t get the added benefits from doing so during character creation. It is important for you to think about what your character was doing before this story began and what kind of “job” they had.

Pick a Career

Take a look at all the Careers listed below and think about the character you are trying to make. Mark all the skills listed as Career Skills. Gain 1 rank in 4 of those skills.

Pick a Specialization

After picking your career, Each has 3 - 6 Specializations. Pick one that fits your character. Mark all the skills listed as Career Skills. Also click the link to autopopulate the talent tree for the charcater sheet. Gain 1 rank in 2 of those skills.
