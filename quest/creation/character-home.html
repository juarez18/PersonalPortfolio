Character Creation

This should provide all information you need to get started on creating your character.

The following steps are as follows:

  1. Determine Character Concept and Background
  2. Determine Starting Obligation
  3. Select Character Species
  4. Select Character Career
  5. Select Specializations
  6. Invest Experience Points
  7. Determine Derived Attributes
  8. Determine Motivations
  9. Choose Gear and Descriptions

The first 2 steps are located on the obligation page.

Career and Specializations are all located on the same page.

And the steps 6 - 9 are all lumped into Investing XP page.
