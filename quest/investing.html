Investing

Overview

Here is where you can spend your experience points. This should have been assigned when you chose your race.

Process

You have lots of options you can: upgrade your characteristics, upgrade your skills, purchase talents, may purchase another specialization. Each will help your character imporve your dice rolls and help your character improve at things.

Investing Experience Points

Options Cost Creation Limits
May spend experience on characteristics. This is the only time characters can increase characteristics with experience points. 10x the purhased rating in experience. May not purhase above 5 during character creation.
May spend to purchase ranks in skills. 5x the purhased rank in experience. Each non-career skill costs an additonal 5. May not purhase above 2 during character creation.
May spend to purchase talents within specializations. Depends in postion in tree. No limits.
May spend to purhase a new specialization. 10x the number of purchased speicalizations including new speicalizations. Non-career specializations cost an addiontal 10xp. No limits.

Determine Attributes

Wound Threshold

Wound Threshold is determined by adding your species starting wound with their brawn. Further increasing brawn rating does not improve wound threshold but can be improved by purchasing talents.

Strain Threshold

Strain Threshold is determined by adding your species starting strain with their willpower rating. Same consraints as above except with willpower.

Defense

Initial defense rating is 0. Defense is typically added by either armor or adopting a defensive positioon in combat. Also some talents increase defense ratings.

Soak Value

A characters soak value is thier brawn rating. Additional soak can be added typically by wearing armor.Any increase in brawn also increases soak

Gear and Appereance

Each PC has a budget of 500 credits.They cannot purhcase any Restricted items. They may keep any credits they don't use and also roll a d100 for credits to start with as well.
